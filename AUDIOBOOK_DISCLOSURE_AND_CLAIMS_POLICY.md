# Audiobook Disclosure And Claims Policy

Earnalism must not claim audiobook availability until the release gate, rights gate, QA gate, and owner approval all pass.

## Allowed Claims Today

- Dracula is available as a controlled reading release.
- Dracula audio is not available yet.
- Internal audiobook model benchmarking may be in progress.

## Blocked Claims Today

- Listen Now.
- Full audiobook available.
- Audiobook included.
- Narrated by any named person or celebrity unless contracted and approved.
- AI voice quality ratings without human listening review.

## Public UX Rule

If audio is disabled, public pages may mention that audio is not available yet, but they must not render playable audio controls or audio purchase claims.

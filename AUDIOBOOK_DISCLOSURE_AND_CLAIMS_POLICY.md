# Audiobook Disclosure And Claims Policy

Earnalism audiobook copy must be truthful, conservative, and approval-gated.

## Required Rules

- If AI-generated or AI-assisted, do not claim human narration.
- If human-recorded, store proof or contract reference internally.
- Public copy must avoid misleading claims.
- Generated narration must be disclosed internally.
- Public disclosure copy must be approved before release.

## Acceptable Public Phrases After Approval

- Premium Bengali Gothic audio preview
- Human-reviewed narration
- Punctuation-aware Bengali narration
- Emotion-sensitive literary audio

## Prohibited Unless Literally True

- No AI touch
- Fully human narrated
- Recorded by professional actor
- Studio recorded
- Official Tagore narration
- Celebrity or public-figure comparisons

## Default Kshudhita Pashan Status

Kshudhita Pashan regenerated narration remains internal only. No public audio copy, player, URL, or Listen Now CTA may appear until the audiobook release gate passes in a separate approval.

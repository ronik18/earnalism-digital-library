# Earnalism Home Screen Spec

## Frame
- Desktop: `1440 x 1200`, ivory/paper background, compact sticky header.
- Tablet: `820 x 1180`, stacked editorial hero.
- Mobile: `390 x 844`, hero copy first, action cards stacked.

## Structure
1. `AppHeader`
2. `HeroEditorial`
3. `CuratedActionCardGroup`
4. `ApprovedAudiobookSpotlight` only when manifest evidence proves approval
5. Bengali and English shelves below fold

## Hero Copy
- Eyebrow: `THE EARNALISM DIGITAL LIBRARY`
- Headline: `A calm digital reading room for timeless Bengali and English literature.`
- Subtitle: reader-ready classics, release-gated audiobooks.
- CTAs: `Start Reading`, `Browse Library`.
- Trust line: `Reader-first editions · Bengali and English classics · Audiobooks only when approved`

## Rules
- Dracula is not the primary hero.
- Bengali Classics, English Classics, and Approved Audiobooks are peer action cards.
- No white text on pale backgrounds.
- No public audio CTA unless endpoint/manifest evidence exists.

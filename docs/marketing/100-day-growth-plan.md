# Earnalism 100-Day Growth Plan

Purpose: build measurable growth for theearnalism.com across social media, ads, owned channels, and reader retention.

Important claim guardrail: "1000X revenue in 100 days" is an aggressive internal stretch target, not a public promise. Public copy should focus on reader value, measurable progress, and truthful product benefits.

Core CTA: Preview first. Pay only for reading time. Read beautifully. Listen deeply.

## Weekly Operating Rhythm

- Monday: analyze prior week metrics and choose three actions.
- Tuesday: publish education content.
- Wednesday: publish reader/book content.
- Thursday: run conversion or retargeting experiment.
- Friday: send email/community touchpoint.
- Saturday: publish short-form video.
- Sunday: document learnings and refresh assets.

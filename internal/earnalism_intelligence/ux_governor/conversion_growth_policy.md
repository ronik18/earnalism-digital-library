# Conversion Growth Policy

Earnalism conversion should come from trust, orientation, and desire to read or listen, not aggressive pressure.

## Conversion Principles

- Customer trust before persuasion.
- Premium calmness before visual noise.
- Reader and listener comfort before decoration.
- Evidence before marketing claims.
- Release truth before conversion copy.

## Preferred Events

- `homepage_cta_click`
- `library_filter_used`
- `book_detail_view`
- `reader_start`
- `listen_start`
- `listen_30_seconds`
- `listen_3_minutes`
- `audio_completion`
- `signup`
- `share_click`
- `return_visit_7d`

## Tracking Rules

- First-party sanitized analytics may run by default.
- Third-party analytics must be opt-in by config or environment.
- Do not track private source URLs, signed URLs, secrets, or unpublished release metadata.

## Copy Rules

- Market reader-ready availability truthfully.
- Market audiobooks only when approved by evidence.
- Reader-only is a positive state, not a defect.

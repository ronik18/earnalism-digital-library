# Typography Policy

Earnalism typography must be calm, smaller, elegant, modern, and readable. Hierarchy should come from spacing, weight, contrast, and rhythm rather than oversized type.

## Scale Targets

- Navigation: 14px-15px.
- Eyebrow: 11px-12px.
- Hero desktop: 42px-52px.
- Hero laptop: 36px-46px.
- Hero mobile: 30px-36px.
- Subtitle: 16px-18px.
- Card title: 18px-22px.
- Card body: 14px-16px.
- Shelf title: 24px-30px.
- Book card title: 15px-17px.
- Metadata: 12px-14px.
- Reader English: 18px-20px.
- Reader Bengali: 19px-21px.

## Line Height

- Hero: 1.08-1.16.
- Body/card: 1.45-1.65.
- English reading: 1.65-1.8.
- Bengali reading: 1.8-2.0.

## Guardrails

- No giant all-caps blocks.
- No cramped Bengali line-height.
- No monotone same-size shelves.
- No white text on pale backgrounds.
- Metadata must remain legible.
- Font reduction must not create accessibility failures.

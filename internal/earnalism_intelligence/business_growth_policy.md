# Business Growth Policy

Earnalism should maximize safe customer-facing availability. Valid readers with premium covers and rights evidence are valuable even when audiobook quality is unresolved.

Prioritize:

- Reader-only Bengali go-live when audiobook quality/provider maturity is blocked.
- Clear availability badges and no broken audio controls.
- Cost-efficient provider experiments before full audiobook generation.
- Titles with clean source, rights, and covers.
- Evidence-backed decisions that protect brand trust.

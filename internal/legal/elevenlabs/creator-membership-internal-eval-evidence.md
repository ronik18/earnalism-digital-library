## Decision Rules

- `HOLD`: evidence is incomplete or still under owner/legal review.
- `ELIGIBLE_INTERNAL_EVAL_ONLY`: one short internal sample may be manually
  generated by the owner in ElevenLabs Studio/UI and imported into the internal
  lab. Public audio and production remain blocked.
- `BLOCKED`: the provider, plan, selected voice, or restrictions are not safe
  for internal evaluation.

## Non-Negotiable Blocks

- Do not use beta services.
- Do not use voice cloning.
- Do not use ElevenReader.
- Do not call the ElevenLabs API from this repository.
- Do not generate full chapters or full audiobooks.
- Do not place audio under `frontend/public` or `frontend/build`.
- Do not expose public audio URLs or public audio metadata.

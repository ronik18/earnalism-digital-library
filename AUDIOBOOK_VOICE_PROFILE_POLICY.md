# Audiobook Voice Profile Policy

Earnalism audiobook voice profiles must be original, consent-safe, and literary.

## Requirements

- No real-person or celebrity imitation.
- Reference/style voices require explicit owner-approved governance.
- Voice profiles must define tone, pacing, emotional restraint, and pronunciation review expectations.
- Public audiobook release requires human listening review and QA pass.

## Dracula English Profile

`english-gothic-premium-v1` is internal review only. It targets a refined female literary narrator with calm Gothic atmosphere and restrained emotion.

## Review Checklist

- Does the voice sound original?
- Does the voice avoid celebrity/person imitation?
- Does the voice remain clear for long listening?
- Does the voice respect punctuation and dialogue without overacting?
- Are pronunciation notes reviewed?
